\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The \doxylink{classtouchgfx_1_1_text_provider}{Text\+Provider} is used in drawing basic strings and strings with one or two wildcards. The \doxylink{classtouchgfx_1_1_text_provider}{Text\+Provider} enables wildcard expansion of the string at the time it is written to the \doxylink{classtouchgfx_1_1_l_c_d}{LCD}.

Wildcards specified as \texorpdfstring{$<$}{<}placeholder\texorpdfstring{$>$}{>} are converted to \doxylink{classtouchgfx_1_1_unicode}{Unicode} value 2 by the text converter tool, and the placeholders are automatically expanded with the specified wildcard buffers at runtime.
