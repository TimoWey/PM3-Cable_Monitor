\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A \doxylink{classtouchgfx_1_1_scrollable_container}{Scrollable\+Container} is a container that allows its contents to be scrolled. It will intercept drag operations and move child nodes accordingly.

A standard \doxylink{classtouchgfx_1_1_container}{Container} will simply clip children that are either larger than the container itself, or children that extend beyond the borders of the container or children that are placed outside the borders of the container. A \doxylink{classtouchgfx_1_1_scrollable_container}{Scrollable\+Container} behaves much like a \doxylink{classtouchgfx_1_1_container}{Container}, except it enables the user to scroll the children and thereby act like a viewport. When the contents of the \doxylink{classtouchgfx_1_1_scrollable_container}{Scrollable\+Container} is scrollable, scrollbars can be seen near the edge of the \doxylink{classtouchgfx_1_1_scrollable_container}{Scrollable\+Container}.

\end{DoxySeeAlso}
\begin{DoxyNote}{Note}
The \doxylink{classtouchgfx_1_1_scrollable_container}{Scrollable\+Container} will consume all Drag\+Events in the area covered by the container. 
\end{DoxyNote}
