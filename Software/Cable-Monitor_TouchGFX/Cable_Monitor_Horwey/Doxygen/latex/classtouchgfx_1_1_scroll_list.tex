For example if the \doxylink{classtouchgfx_1_1_scroll_list}{Scroll\+List} is 200, each drawable is 50 and distance before and distance after are 25, then there is room for three visible drawables inside the \doxylink{classtouchgfx_1_1_scroll_list}{Scroll\+List}. When scrolling, part of the scrolled out drawables can be seen before and after the three drawables. In this case 25/50 = 50\% of a drawable can be seen before and after the three drawables in the \doxylink{classtouchgfx_1_1_scroll_list}{Scroll\+List}.
