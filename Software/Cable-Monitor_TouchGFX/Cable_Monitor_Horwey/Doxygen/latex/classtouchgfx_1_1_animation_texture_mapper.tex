\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A \doxylink{classtouchgfx_1_1_texture_mapper}{Texture\+Mapper} with animation capabilities. Note that the angles of the \doxylink{classtouchgfx_1_1_texture_mapper}{Texture\+Mapper} is normalized to lie in the range \mbox{[}0; 2PI\mbox{[} at the beginning at the animation. The end angles should be relative to this and are limited to values in the range \mbox{[}-\/32.\+7; 32.\+7\mbox{]}.
