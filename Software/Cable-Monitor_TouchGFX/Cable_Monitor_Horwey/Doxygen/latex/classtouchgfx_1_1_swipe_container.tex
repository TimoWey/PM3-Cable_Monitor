\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A \doxylink{classtouchgfx_1_1_swipe_container}{Swipe\+Container} is a \doxylink{classtouchgfx_1_1_container}{Container} with a horizontally laid out list of identically sized Drawables. The bottom of the \doxylink{classtouchgfx_1_1_swipe_container}{Swipe\+Container} shows a page indicator to indicate the position in the horizontal list of items in the \doxylink{classtouchgfx_1_1_swipe_container}{Swipe\+Container}.
